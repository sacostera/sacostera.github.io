The house

Our 400-year-old townhouse offers space, charm, and a relaxed atmosphere in the heart of Fornalutx. With 3 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, several terraces and a garden, it’s ideal for families or friends.

Location

Located in one of Mallorca’s most beautiful villages, surrounded by the Tramuntana mountains. Walk to cafés, restaurants, and shops. The beach at Port de Sóller is just a short drive away.

Good to know

  • Access involves a short walk with stairs
  • No air conditioning (fans available)
  • Best suited for guests comfortable with stairs
